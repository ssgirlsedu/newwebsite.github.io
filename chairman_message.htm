CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

It is my immense pleasure to see the institute as a primer center for Maritime and Education. The journey of last fifteen years has placed the institute in the category of big institute in India. The has happened only by the collective efforts of our esteem clients, ship owners and the trust of dear parents. Today, I can stand and say that the institute has been imparting the training and courses from a entrant to master rank. The activities are now increases at a large. The cooperation, coordination and trust of our dear students, faculties and staff has made us as No 1 institute in Delhi. We are sincerely thankful to all.

Our efforts are to provide the maximum courses in the New Delhi as for many management level courses are conducted in the western part of the country, hence these officers has to travel to the other part. In this process, a lot of time is spent in attending and travelling for the courses. The institute has started AFF, PSCRB and refresher courses and a large response received from the seafarers. This shows that the institute carry a good reputation and student friendly teachers, facilities and in time delivery of the documents.

For the ease of our new entrant of Deck cadets and Ratings, the institute operates a Recruitment and placement License so as to provide them onboard training soon completion of their pre sea course in the institute. As a results today almost all students are employed in the world class shipping companies. We are working on more tie ups with Indian and foreign principals so as all students can join a ship.

Home like food, accommodation and family like environment are the main features of ours. We assure all the parents of our student that the institute shall always keep the training and comfort of our students right on the top without any compromises on the standards of training and regulation of DG Shipping. I prefer to share with all our prospective seafarers and their near n dears, parents and well wishers that the institute has the institute has a laid down procedure of assessing the students on their performance. These students has shown very good results in examinations conducted by the Board of examination and Indian maritime university and I assure that the institute shall always keep this trend intact.

I personally thankful to all those have brought laurels to this institute by their dedication, hard work and trust. We will come up with new heights as we are commencing new courses for Engineering officers also very soon.

Affectionate yours,

Manjeet Lochav

(Chairman)
