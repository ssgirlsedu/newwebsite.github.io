DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

As it is said that "Good better best, never rest till good is better and better is best". Continious efforts are the key to success and there is no substitute of hard work. The objectives of our are and shall always remain to impart the best education and training to our all course participants as per the very best directives of the Indian administration. This institute started with an objective to spread the marine education and training not only in India, but over the world. The efforts are still on and today we are meeting the needs of modern shipping industry as per their requirements. We are providing them well trained officers, crew and maritime managers. We are thankful to all those involved in the process of achieving our goals and to bring this institute on the highest level in the field of education and training including the on board training. We are greatly obliged to our dear clients, ship owners and the industry managers for showing their trust and belief in us. We are also thankful to the parents, faculties, instructors and the staff for their efforts, dedication and hard work.

Today, the institue also operate the DG Shipping approved license for Recruitment and Placement of seafarers. We assure our all prospective students on their further onboard training. We are leaving no stone unturned to secure jobs for our pre sea course students. The placements are the backbone of any training institute and the future of the institute and students depends upon the placements only. I promised the best training, comfort and job security to our passed out and current batch students.

I extend my congratulations to our all passed out students, those are sailing around the globe and earned name and fame. Today our cadets are even in line to attain the rank of Masters, it really gives us the utmost pleasure and I expect the same from our all course participants. I am extremely thankful to our foreign and Indian Principals those have shown their trust by signing the proper contracts to provide the jobs to our students. I further assure that we will strive to sharp our training to produce the best officers and crew for the shipping industry and I am sure, to meet such objectives, we need the blessing of elders, dedication of teachers and trust of parents.

I wish all the very best seafaring career ahead to our all students and my best wishes to the parents.

JAI HIND

(Joginder Singh)

Executive Director
