Pre Sea DNS

Information on DNS Course 01.08.2021 – Sriram Institute of Marine Studies

Institute shall commence the DNS Course with an intake of 40 cadets from 01.08.2022. The course is approved by the DG Shipping and affiliated to the Indian Maritime University. {IMU} Chennai.

Course Intake

40 students

Course scheduled from

01.08.2022

Duration of course

First 02 semester at institute campus, 03 semester on board

Eligibility criteria to attend the course

(a) Pass class 10+2 or its equalling from a recognised board by the Central Govt. of India or state Govt./NIOS with 50% marks in English.

(b) have minimum 60% in aggregate of Physics, Chemistry and Maths aggregate in class 12 (10+2)

(c ) BSc Students have 50% in BSc course and Physics be one of the subject in any year.

(d) Relaxation in marks PCM; 05% relaxation to the students falls under SC/ST category. The certificate shall be required at the time of counselling.

Age limit

Minimum 17 years and maximum 25 years as on the date course commences

(Age relaxation as per existing rules of Govt. of India)

Medical standard

Both eyes 6/6 with no aids and no colour blindness, no deformity in body, no stemming, No any kind of handicapped is accepted in course.Fit for sea service

Requirement of passport

Able to present passport with in 10 days of commencement of course for process of INDoS application and batch details.

Adhaar card

students to submit coloured scan copy of their Adhaar card for admission.

EWS/Category certificate

Student seeking any reservation as EWS/SC/ST/OBC etc. are to obtain a fresh certificate from the Govt. authorities and shall deposit in campus at the time of admission/counselling for course in institute.

Course fees

(a) IMU Counselling and Programme fees : INR 35,000 {To be paid on passing Online IMU CET only

(a) First Semester Fees: 1,55,000/- (To be paid on commencement/ entry in Institute)

(b) Second semester fees : 1,55,000/ 2nd semester by 15.02.2022

Security deposit

INR 10,000/- (ten thousand only to meet exam fees and laundry charges

Procedure

Indian Maritime University has now announced an Online All India Common Entrance Test. Participant are to register for Online Common Entrance examination and rank card shall be issued based on merit in online test or percentile. The test shall be based on syllabus of 12th standard English, reasoning. maths, chemistry physics etc.

Requirement of laptop/desk top

Preparative classes for IMU CET2021 shall be conducted by our officers on declaration of programme on subject. Dayes shall be announced for CET predation. Ensure you have Desktop or laptop only as the CET cannot be done on mobile. Mock drill on CET shall be conducted.

Preparative training on CET to registered student

The institute shall conduct an Online session to complete CET syllabus through Online mode for registered students from 16.08.2021 to 28.08.2021.
